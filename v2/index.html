Better Crash Reporting

27.05.2020 00:00
Crash events are some of the more serious events that can happen when operating a service. Usually it is crashing components that cause cascading failures and service outages. It is critical to have good visibility into those events, since they can cause major damage and should be avoided. Unfortunately, debugging crashes is one of the more complicated endeavors.
